SIMPLE INTEREST


Simple Interest Calculator

Formula

The formula to calculate Simple Interest is:

(P x R x T)/100

Or if the rate is converted to decimal from %, then the Simple Interest is:

P x R x T

Where:

  • P is the principal amount
  • R is the interest rate
  • T is the time involved in years

Example

Ariel takes a loan of $8,000 to buy a used truck at the rate of 9 % simple Interest.Calculate the annual interest to be paid for the loan amount.

Solution:
From the details given in the problem Principle = P = $8,000 and R = 9% or 0.09 expressed as a decimal.
As the annual Interest is to be calculated, the time period T =1.
Plugging these values in the simple Interest formula,
I = P x T x R
= 8,000 x 1 x 0.09
= 720.00
Annual Interest to be paid = $720
